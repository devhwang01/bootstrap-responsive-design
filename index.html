About Me

I'm Devon Hwang, an interactive design student at James Madison University passionate about crafting user-centered visual experiences. My work blends creative tools like Adobe InDesign, Illustrator, and Photoshop with technical skills in HTML/CSS, prototyping, and research. I grew up in Florida with a love for the beach, and I currently live in Ashburn, Virginia. I enjoy spending my free time with my family and friends, enjoying the outdoors, and going to the gym. I am always looking for new opportunities to learn and grow in the field of design.

Design with Purpose

My design philosophy focuses on clarity, empathy, and usability. Whether I'm designing with Adobe tools or developing with HTML/CSS, I aim to create digital experiences that support real users and solve real problems.

From user research to high-fidelity prototypes, I enjoy working through the entire creative process to make projects that feel polished and purposeful. Collaboration, iteration, and thoughtful design are at the core of my work.
